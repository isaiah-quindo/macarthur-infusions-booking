# Collection Notice

**Version:** 2026-06-14

This short notice tells you what happens to the personal and health information you provide when you book an appointment with Macarthur Infusions. It satisfies our obligation under Australian Privacy Principle 5.

## What we collect

Your name, email, phone, the service you book, and any notes you provide about your health, medications, or treatment goals.

## Why we collect it

To provide the service you book, manage your appointment, process payment, send confirmations and reminders, and meet our legal obligations.

## Who we share it with

- **Square** — to process online payments.
- **Resend** — to send booking emails on our behalf.
- **Amazon Web Services (Sydney)** — to host the booking system in Australia.

We do not use or share your information for marketing.

## Where it's stored

In Australia (AWS Sydney). Your contact details and treatment notes are encrypted in our database.

## Your rights

You can access your information, ask us to correct it, or complain about how we have handled it — email **macarthurinfusions@gmail.com**. You can also complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner at <https://www.oaic.gov.au>.
