## 2. What we collect

When you book an appointment with us, we collect:

- **Identifiers and contact details:** your name, email address, phone number.
- **Health and service information:** the service you book, any notes you provide about your health conditions, medications, allergies, or treatment goals.
- **Booking and payment metadata:** appointment date and time, payment method, payment reference, and timestamps related to your booking.
- **Consent record:** the version of this Privacy Policy and Collection Notice you agreed to, the date and time you agreed, your IP address, and your browser's user-agent string.
- **Technical information:** server logs (IP address, request path, timestamps) needed to operate and secure the site.

We do not deliberately collect more sensitive information (e.g. ethnicity, religious beliefs, sexual orientation). If you choose to mention such information in the "notes" field, we will treat it with the same protections as your health information.

We do not currently capture date of birth. If we add this later (for example, to apply minor-retention rules), we will update this policy.

## 3. Why we collect it

We collect your information for the following primary purposes:

- To provide the health service you book.
- To manage your booking — confirmations, reminders, rescheduling, cancellation.
- To process payment for your booking.
- To meet our legal and professional obligations (record-keeping, tax, complaint handling).
- To improve and secure the booking system.

We do **not** use your contact details for marketing.

## 4. Who we share it with

We share your information only with the providers we need to run the service. Each provider is bound by a written agreement and their own privacy obligations.

- **Square** (payment processor) — when you pay online, your card details are entered directly into Square's hosted payment page; we never see them. Square receives your name, email, and the booking reference for the transaction.
- **Resend** (transactional email provider) — sends booking confirmations, reminders, and cancellation notices on our behalf. Receives your name and email address.
- **Amazon Web Services, Sydney region (ap-southeast-2)** — hosts the booking system and database in Australia.

We do **not** sell your information, and we do **not** share it with marketing partners.

We may disclose information without your consent where we are required or permitted to by law — for example, under court order or to investigate suspected unlawful activity.

## 5. Where it's stored

All booking and patient data is stored on servers located in Australia (AWS Sydney region). Backups are encrypted and stored in the same region.

When you pay online, Square may process and store the transaction in accordance with its own privacy notice, which you should review at <https://squareup.com/au/en/legal/general/privacy>.

## 6. How we protect it

We apply security controls appropriate for health information, including:

- **Encryption in transit:** all traffic to the booking site is served over TLS (HTTPS).
- **Encryption at rest:** your name, email, phone, and notes are encrypted in our database with keys we control.
- **Access control:** clinical staff can see your information only through authenticated admin accounts; logins are rate-limited and password-protected.
- **Audit logging** of administrative access to bookings (rolling out — see our Privacy & Compliance Plan).
- **Secure backups**, tested for restoration.

No system is perfectly secure. If a data breach occurs that is likely to result in serious harm, we will notify you and the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) as required by the Notifiable Data Breaches scheme.

## 7. How long we keep it

| Information | Retention period | Then |
|---|---|---|
| Booking records (adults) | At least 7 years from the date of your last appointment | De-identified or deleted |
| Booking records (minors) | Until the patient turns 25 | De-identified or deleted |
| Payment records | 7 years (Australian Taxation Office requirement) | Financial fields retained, personal identifiers de-identified |
| Audit logs | At least 7 years | Retained |

When information is de-identified, we keep aggregate fields (service, date, status) that have no link to you, and we destroy the personal identifiers.

## 8. Your rights

Under the Privacy Act and NSW Health Records and Information Privacy Act, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal and health information that we hold.
- **Correct** information that is inaccurate, out-of-date, incomplete, or misleading.
- **Complain** about how we have handled your information.

To make a request, email **macarthurinfusions@gmail.com** or write to us at the postal address above. We will respond within 30 days. There is no fee for making a request, though we may charge a small fee for the actual cost of producing copies of records.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you can complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner:

- Web: <https://www.oaic.gov.au/privacy/privacy-complaints>
- Phone: 1300 363 992

NSW residents can also complain to the NSW Information and Privacy Commission: <https://www.ipc.nsw.gov.au>.
